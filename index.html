3D Printing

3D printing is a technology that turns a digital file into a real object. The material is added layer by layer, which makes it possible to create both simple and very complex shapes. The advantages are speed, precision and adaptability.

3D Modeling

3D modeling is the creation of a digital model ready for printing. Models can be made from scratch or based on sketches and ideas. Professionally prepared model ensures precision and functionality.
