Engineering ideas into reality

Strataform Innovations is a 3D printing and modeling studio that helps makers, engineers and businesses turn digital files into precise, physical parts.

OUR STORY

Built around the print, not just for it

Strataform Innovations started with a simple idea: getting from a digital model to a finished physical part should be fast, transparent and precise — without needing to be an expert in 3D printing yourself.

We work with calibrated FDM printers and a growing library of materials and finishes, so every part we ship is checked against the specification it was ordered against, not just "close enough."

Whether it's a one-off concept model or a production run of functional parts, our process stays the same: understand the part, recommend the right material, print it right, and stand behind it.

WHAT DRIVES US

The principles behind every print

Four things we hold every project to, from the first file upload to the part landing in your hands.

01

Precision

Every model is checked for printability before it goes anywhere near a printer, so tolerances and fit are right the first time.

02

Speed

Calibrated machines and a clear quoting process mean less waiting and fewer surprises between your file and your part.

03

Transparency

You know the material, the finish and the price up front — and we'll tell you plainly if something isn't printable as designed.

04

Partnership

We iterate with you, not just for you — from first prototype to the version that's ready for real-world use.
